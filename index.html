SkillSwap

SkillSwap is an opportunity for everyone to learn from each other, no matter what skills they have. It is designed for school students who want extra help, women who wish to learn and share knowledge, and professionals who want to exchange their expertise. Most importantly, this platform gives people with disabilities or those who cannot easily go outside the chance to learn and teach online. By connecting together, people from all walks of life can share their knowledge, support one another, and grow their skills without barriers.
